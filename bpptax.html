WHAT WE DO

Tangible personal property that is used to produce income is subject to taxation in the state of Texas. Tangible personal property includes such things as furniture, fixtures, inventories, equipment, motor vehicles, vessels, and aircraft.

Mees Sandhu CPAs offer business personal property tax consulting for Texas businesses. We can help you fill in the rendition forms and submit them accordingly. Our team gets to know yours well and deeply, so we can apply our experience to your circumstances in a detailed way.
